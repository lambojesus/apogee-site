About Apogee

Category-defining retailers in markets others ignore.

Apogee Stores LLC is a cutting-edge leader in the specialty tire, battery and accessories industry. Ranked #400 among the prestigious Inc. 5000 fastest growing private companies in America, our commitment to excellence and innovation is reflected in our diverse portfolio of niche websites, each catering to specific needs and preferences of its amazing and passionate customers.

Since establishing Golf Cart Tire Supply in 2014, Apogee has set the standard for high-quality golf cart tires, wheels, and accessories retailing online. The company has expanded methodically launching the wheel and tire brand TREX in 2016, engineering Golf Cart Sellers (the world's first p2p Golf Cart marketplace) in 2022, and now recognizing the demand and missing link for a quality retailer of lawn mower tires, we are launching Lawn Mower Tire Supply in 2026, further pushing limits in the specialty tire space.

Our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction and product quality drives our success and fuels our growth. Every decision we make is filtered through one lens: does this make us more important to the people we serve?
